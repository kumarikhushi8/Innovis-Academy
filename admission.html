Admissions

Admissions open for the academic year 2025-26. Apply now to secure your future at Innovis Academy.

Admissions Process

Step 1: Virtual Tour

Explore Innovis Academy from the comfort of your home. Take a virtual tour to experience our modern classrooms, advanced labs, and vibrant student spaces.

Take Virtual Tour

Step 2: Inquiry Form

Fill out our online inquiry form to receive a detailed consultation. Our admissions team will guide you through the next steps based on your child's age and requirements.

Submit Inquiry

Step 3: Admission Consultation

After submitting the inquiry form, our team will contact you to schedule a virtual meeting. This session will introduce you to our school culture, curriculum, and values, followed by a Q&A session.

Step 4: Registration

Once you've decided to join Innovis Academy, fill out the registration form to proceed with the admission process.

Register Now

Step 5: Student Interaction

For students from Pre-KG to Grade 9, we arrange an interaction session where your child gets to engage with our faculty and experience our student-friendly learning environment.

Step 6: Admission Confirmation

Once the required documents are reviewed and the seat is confirmed, you will receive an official admission confirmation letter via email. Parents must accept the offer and complete the fee payment within the given timeframe.

Step 7: Parent Orientation

At the beginning of the academic year, parents are invited to an orientation program. This session provides insights into Innovis Academy's teaching methods, policies, and school environment.
